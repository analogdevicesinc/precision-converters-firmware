    EVB Discovery Firmware

    Analog Devices Inc. Evaluation Board Discovery IIO based Firmware

    About

    This embedded firmware is used to extract the hardware board (or EVB) information from the EEPROM and send it over to the IIO client to discover the user connected EVB. The board information is passed in the form of IIO context attributes to IIO client.
